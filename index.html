The Problem

The Product

A conversational AI that checks in daily, sends gentle reminders, and offers real emotional presence, without being patronizing. Designed for low cognitive effort and high trust.

  • Evaluating conversational AI approaches for the core experience

Philosophy

How I think

01

Simple over clever

If I have to explain how to use it, it's not ready. The best product experiences feel obvious in hindsight, which is the hardest thing to build.

02

Useful before beautiful

Design matters, but it has to earn its place. I start with whether something actually works for a real person before thinking about how it looks.

03

Retention tells the truth

Anyone can get a download. The honest metric is whether someone came back the next day. Retention is where real product feedback lives.

04

Products need to pay for themselves

A product that can't sustain itself can't keep helping people. I think about pricing and monetization early, not as an afterthought.

How I work
